The Leaf Infection Classifier is a web-based application that can determine whether a leaf is healthy or diseased, and if diseased, what type of disease it has. It is based on a machine learning model that has been trained on a large dataset of leaf images, and is able to accurately classify the images into one of several categories of diseases.

This application can be used by farmers, researchers, and anyone who is interested in plant health to quickly and easily identify potential problems with their plants, and take corrective action before the problem becomes too severe.
